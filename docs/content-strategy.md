# Content Strategy

## Purpose
This document defines the content strategy for the BuiltExpert marketing website, including page-by-page content goals, copy approach, messaging priorities, and CTA strategy.

This is a content and conversion document. Its job is to make sure every page has a clear role in moving the contractor visitor toward trust, qualification, and action.

---

## 1. Content Strategy Goals

The website content should help BuiltExpert:

- explain what the business does in clear language
- attract qualified contractors
- build trust quickly
- communicate contractor-specific relevance without sounding gimmicky
- show that BuiltExpert understands business outcomes, not just websites
- reduce poor-fit inquiries
- move visitors toward discovery calls or audit inquiries

---

## 2. Core Messaging Principles

### Lead With Outcome
Content should focus on:
- more qualified leads
- better conversion
- stronger credibility
- clearer local presence
- less wasted spend
- support after launch

### Stay Clear And Human
Tone should be:
- clear
- confident
- direct
- practical

### Make The Offer Easy To Understand
The visitor should quickly understand:
- what BuiltExpert does
- who it helps
- why it is different
- what happens next

### Sell With Structure, Not Hype
The content should build trust through:
- contractor relevance
- proof
- process
- offer clarity
- expectation setting

---

## 3. Core Brand Message

### Primary Message
BuiltExpert helps contractors get more qualified leads through websites, landing pages, local SEO, and contractor-focused growth systems.

### Supporting Differentiators
- contractor-specific messaging
- built to convert leads
- practical audit and growth path
- clear process
- support after launch

---

## 4. CTA Strategy

## Primary CTA
**Book A Call**

## Secondary CTA
**View The Audit**

### Supporting CTAs
- Contact Us
- See How It Works
- Explore Services
- Start With An Audit

### CTA Rules
- one primary CTA focus per page
- keep CTA language direct
- match CTA to user intent
- every major page should have at least one meaningful next step

---

## 5. Copy Tone

### Tone Attributes
- Clear
- Confident
- Helpful
- Direct
- Contractor-friendly
- Non-corporate

### Avoid
- marketing jargon
- vague agency filler
- exaggerated claims
- empty positioning language

### Preferred Writing Style
- short paragraphs
- strong headlines
- plain English
- outcome-first language
- specific claims over vague adjectives

---

## 6. Page-By-Page Content Plan

## Home
Goal: act as the main conversion page and brand narrative page.  
Must do:
- explain BuiltExpert quickly
- establish contractor relevance
- show service categories
- provide proof
- explain process
- direct users toward a call or audit

Primary CTA: Book A Call  
Secondary CTA: View The Audit

## Services
Goal: help users understand what BuiltExpert offers and where they fit.  
Must do:
- explain each service area clearly
- reduce confusion around what is offered
- guide users toward the right engagement path

Primary CTA: Book A Call  
Secondary CTA: Explore Specific Service

## Service Detail Pages
Goal: convert users with specific service intent.  
Must do:
- define the service
- explain who it is for
- explain outcomes
- reduce objections
- show supporting proof
- move user to contact

Primary CTA: Book A Call  
Secondary CTA: View Related Work

## Audit Page
Goal: provide a lower-risk entry offer.  
Must do:
- explain what the audit is
- explain what the contractor gets
- show why it matters
- create a logical path to inquiry or call

Primary CTA: Start The Audit  
Secondary CTA: Book A Call

## Work / Case Studies
Goal: provide proof and reduce perceived risk.  
Must do:
- show relevant contractor projects or growth examples
- demonstrate range and relevance
- reinforce credibility

Primary CTA: Start Your Project  
Secondary CTA: View Case Study

## Process
Goal: show that BuiltExpert works in a structured, low-chaos way.  
Primary CTA: Book A Call

## Pricing
Goal: frame value and investment without becoming a cheap rate card.  
Primary CTA: Discuss Your Project

## About
Goal: make the brand feel credible, real, and aligned with contractors.  
Primary CTA: Start A Conversation

## Contact
Goal: turn warm intent into qualified inquiries.  
Primary CTA: Submit Inquiry / Book A Call

## Thank You
Goal: confirm the action and reduce uncertainty.

---

## 7. Internal Linking Strategy
Content should support natural movement between:
- homepage and services
- homepage and audit
- services and detail pages
- work and case studies
- pricing and contact
- about and contact

---

## 8. Final Recommendation
The content strategy should stay focused on one thing: making BuiltExpert easier to trust, easier to understand, and easier to hire.

If a piece of copy does not improve clarity, proof, qualification, or conversion, it probably does not need to be there.
