# Editorial Calendar

## Purpose
This document defines the post-launch publishing roadmap for BuiltExpert content. It exists to keep the website from becoming static after launch and to guide what should be published next based on business value.

This is not a full content marketing machine yet. It is a practical publishing plan.

---

## 1. Editorial Goals

The content roadmap should help BuiltExpert:
- strengthen proof over time
- improve service-page depth
- support SEO growth
- support outbound and paid campaigns
- sharpen positioning through repeated relevant content

---

## 2. Content Priorities

## Highest Priority
1. Case studies
2. Service detail pages
3. FAQ expansion
4. Offer / landing pages
5. Trade pages

## Medium Priority
6. Educational blog posts
7. Resource / lead magnet pages

## Lower Priority Early On
8. Broad thought leadership
9. Generalist blog content with weak buyer intent

---

## 3. First 90-Day Publishing Plan

## Month 1
### Focus
Launch stabilization + proof

### Recommended Content
- publish or refine first featured case study
- improve homepage proof blocks
- finalize 1–2 service detail pages
- add missing FAQs from real sales questions

## Month 2
### Focus
Service depth

### Recommended Content
- publish additional service detail page(s)
- publish second case study
- create one offer/landing page if there is active outreach or ads
- refine pricing content based on inquiry quality

## Month 3
### Focus
Search and niche authority support

### Recommended Content
- publish third case study
- publish first educational or evaluation-style article
- identify one trade page opportunity if niche focus is sharpening
- create internal linking connections across published content

---

## 4. Content Types And Cadence

| Content Type | Suggested Early Cadence | Priority |
|---|---:|---:|
| Case Studies | 1 per month if possible | High |
| Service Detail Pages | 1–2 per month until complete | High |
| FAQ Updates | Ongoing | High |
| Offer / Landing Pages | As needed for campaigns | High |
| Trade Pages | As niche strategy sharpens | High |
| Blog Posts | 1–2 per month after core pages are strong | Medium |
| Resources / Lead Magnets | As needed for list building | Medium |

## 6. Content Selection Criteria

Before publishing, ask:
- does this support a real offer?
- does this help the right buyer trust BuiltExpert?
- does this match search or campaign intent?
- does this make the site more useful commercially?

If not, it is probably not the next best content to create.

---

## 7. Publishing Workflow

1. choose next content item based on business priority
2. draft content
3. review positioning and CTA alignment
4. review SEO structure if relevant
5. publish
6. add internal links
7. track impact

---

## 8. Recommendation

Do not try to “do content marketing” broadly right away.

The smartest editorial plan for BuiltExpert is:
- publish stronger proof
- deepen service relevance
- support real contractor buyer questions
- create pages that help sell, rank, and convert

That will compound faster than generic posting.
