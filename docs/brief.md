# Project Brief

## Project Name
BuiltExpert Marketing Website

## Project Summary
BuiltExpert needs a marketing website that acts as the brand’s primary sales asset. The site should clearly explain what the business does, who it serves, why it is different, and how qualified contractors can take the next step.

The project is not just about having an online presence. It is about creating a website that improves positioning, trust, lead quality, and conversion.

## Background
Many contractors either rely on weak websites that do not convert or on lead generation channels they do not control. A lot of marketing providers promise growth but speak in vague language and do not understand how contractors actually buy marketing: they want more qualified leads, clearer ROI, and less wasted time.

BuiltExpert is positioned to solve that gap with contractor-specific websites, landing pages, local SEO, and lead system support designed around real buying behavior and field-service realities.

## Primary Goals
- turn qualified traffic into discovery calls
- generate contractor audit inquiries
- improve trust and clarity for new visitors
- create a stronger market position in contractor marketing
- support referrals, outbound outreach, SEO, and paid traffic
- establish a scalable foundation for future niche and city pages

## Secondary Goals
- showcase contractor-relevant proof
- explain services and engagement paths clearly
- reduce time spent manually explaining the offer on calls
- filter out poor-fit leads earlier
- support future case study and content marketing efforts

## Problem Statement
Without a strong marketing website, BuiltExpert relies too heavily on one-to-one explanation and manual sales effort. That weakens positioning, slows sales, and makes it harder to create a repeatable pipeline.

The site needs to absorb part of the sales burden by answering key questions, establishing trust, and guiding the right contractors toward contact.

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience
Growth-minded contractors and home service businesses that:
- want more qualified leads
- need a stronger online presence
- are tired of weak or generic websites
- want marketing that understands the trades
- value straightforward process and measurable outcomes

### Secondary Audience
Specialty trade owners and growing contractors that:
- need a better website or landing page system
- want local SEO support
- may need help with an audit and growth plan first
- want support beyond launch

## Ideal Visitor Mindset
The ideal visitor already knows they need better lead flow, better conversion, or a stronger online presence. They are trying to find a provider that understands contractors and can help them get better business results without agency fluff.

## Core Message
BuiltExpert helps contractors get more qualified leads through websites, landing pages, local SEO, and contractor-focused growth systems.

## Key Differentiators
- contractor-specific messaging
- built to convert leads, not just look decent
- practical audit and growth path
- clear process
- local lead-generation alignment
- support after launch

## Success Metrics

### Primary KPIs
- discovery call bookings
- qualified audit/contact submissions

### Secondary KPIs
- CTA click-through rate
- case study engagement
- service page engagement
- scroll depth on key pages
- bounce rate improvements on primary entry pages

### Conversion Events To Track
- `view_page`
- `click_cta`
- `form_start`
- `form_submit`
- `phone_click`
- `booking_complete`

## Strategic Outcome
The website should make BuiltExpert feel like a serious, contractor-focused growth brand with a real system behind it. It should help the business attract better-fit clients, improve sales efficiency, and create a strong foundation for long-term growth.
